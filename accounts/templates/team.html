Welcome to MSA TRU

Connect, Engage, and Thrive!

Welcome to the official website of the Thompson Rivers University Muslim Students Association (MSA TRU). We are a vibrant and diverse community dedicated to fostering an inclusive and welcoming environment for Muslim students at TRU. Here, you'll find information, resources, and a sense of belonging that will enhance your university experience.

Who We Are:

At MSA TRU, our mission is to provide a support system for Muslim students, helping them connect with one another, engage in meaningful activities, and thrive academically and socially. Our organization is led by a dedicated team of executive members who are committed to creating a positive impact within the TRU community.

Ready to get started? Explore the rest of our website to learn more about MSA TRU and how you can become part of our dynamic community. We look forward to welcoming you!
